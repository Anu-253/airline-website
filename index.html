What is the refund policy?
Most Airgo fares are refundable depending on the fare class. Fully refundable fares allow cancellations with no penalty, while discounted or promotional fares may be non-refundable or incur a cancellation fee. Refunds are processed to the original payment method and may take 5‚Äì10 business days depending on the bank. What's the luggage allowance?
Luggage allowance varies by route and fare class. For most standard fares, Airgo includes one carry-on (up to 7kg) and one checked bag (up to 23kg). Overweight, oversized, and extra bags incur additional charges which can be added at booking or at check-in. Always check your booking confirmation for the exact allowance tied to your ticket.
